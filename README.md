# AI-based-Anamoly-detection-platform
🏋️‍♂️ FitBuddy AI
Your Personalized AI-Powered Fitness & Nutrition Coach

FitBuddy AI is an intelligent fitness plan generator built for the Smart Intern program. It leverages the power of the Google Gemini 2.5 Flash model and Streamlit to provide users with customized 7-day workout and meal plans based on their unique profiles.

✨ Features
Personalized Planning: Generates 7-day workout and meal routines based on age, weight, goals, and available equipment.

Goal-Oriented Diets: Tailored nutritional advice to match your fitness journey (Bulking, Cutting, or Maintenance).

AI-Powered Insights: Uses Google Gemini for high-quality, conversational, and accurate fitness guidance.

User-Friendly Interface: A clean, responsive web UI built with Streamlit.

Safety First: Includes built-in medical disclaimers to ensure user safety.
